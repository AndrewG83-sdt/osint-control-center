# Ethical Use Disclaimer

OSINT Control Center is intended for legitimate work with public sources, authorized investigations, defensive research, journalism, threat intelligence, and case management where an appropriate legal basis exists.

Do not use this project for stalking, harassment, doxxing, unauthorized surveillance, credential theft, evasion, or collection of personal data without a lawful basis.

The application is local-first, but when you open generated searches, the query terms are sent to third-party services such as search engines, social platforms, and maps. Use test data or authorized case data only.

You are responsible for complying with applicable laws, platform terms, rate limits, organizational policy, and privacy requirements in your jurisdiction.
